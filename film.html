Kim Chiu Filmography
 



Love Spell.

In the tradition of hit romantic series like Mr. Kupido, Flames and Your Song, ABS-CBN introduces a new kind of romantic adventure through Love Spell. Be enchanted and get inspired by the month-long love stories that have the feel of popular romantic comedies such as Freaky Friday and She's The Man.

Starring the freshest and brightest young stars today, Love Spell is not only geared towards entertainment, but it also shares valuable life lessons for people who love being in love. It's really a refreshing treat to spend your lazy Sunday afternoons with.

Feel the love and get caught in a Love Spell every Sunday afternoon after Your Song.
 



Aalug alug - Television Sitcom

When life gives you lemons, you make lemonade. But what if you've signed up for a job as a dancer or a bartender but end up being a janitor in Japan? Meet Johnny, Banjoe and Jayson, three guys who wanted to make their lives better by going abroad but ends up getting tricked by their scheming agent, Keanna - played by PBB Celebrity Big Winner Keanna Reeves. What happens to them abroad is a series of unfortunate and wacky events but the fun really starts once they return to the Philippines and come looking for Keanna.

She makes a deal with the three guys that she'll let them stay in her house - a condemned 3-storey building, in exchange for their forgiveness. John Prats plays Johnny Montejo the pseudo nonvirgin and the "dancing dynamite" of the three boys. Banjoe Rosales played by Zanjoe Marudo is one of his best buds. He is the trouble magnet hunk of the group. Jason Gainza, plays Jayson Giniza the 30-year old virgin houseboy of Doña Etang who tries to pass himself off as a gigolo.

Watch out for this hilarious and one-of-a-kind sitcom.
